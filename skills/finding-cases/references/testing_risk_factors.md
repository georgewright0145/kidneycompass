# CKD testing risk factors (reference for finding-cases)

Canonical source: `spec/guidelines/acr_collection_and_lifestyle.md` (NICE case-finding, PRD §4.10).
Case-finding, not diagnosis. Pending UK renal-clinician sign-off.

Prompt an **undiagnosed** at-risk person to ask their GP for an **eGFR blood test and a urine ACR
(mg/mmol)** if they report any of:

- Diabetes
- Hypertension (high blood pressure)
- Cardiovascular disease
- Prior acute kidney injury (AKI)
- Family history of kidney disease
- Relevant long-term medicines

This routes toward the test and the clinician who diagnoses. Never state or imply the person already
has CKD.
