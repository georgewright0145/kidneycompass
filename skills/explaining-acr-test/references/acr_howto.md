# How to collect an ACR correctly (reference for explaining-acr-test)

Canonical source: `spec/guidelines/acr_collection_and_lifestyle.md` (NICE NG203). Education, not a
home diagnostic. Pending UK renal-clinician sign-off.

- **First-morning sample preferred.**
- **Avoid vigorous exercise for 24 hours** before.
- **Do NOT collect during** menstruation, UTI, fever, or other acute illness (falsely raise ACR).
- New result **3–70 mg/mmol** → confirm on a repeat first-morning sample.
- Result **≥ 70 mg/mmol** → no repeat needed.
- **ACR ≥ 3 mg/mmol** is clinically important proteinuria.
- Units: **mg/mmol** (UK). Cross-ref mg/g only when a KDIGO threshold is quoted.
