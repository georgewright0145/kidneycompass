## G categories (eGFR ml/min/1.73m², inclusive lower bound)
G1 ≥90 · G2 60–89 · G3a 45–59 · G3b 30–44 · G4 15–29 · G5 <15

## A categories (ACR mg/mmol; A2 boundary inclusive, A3 strictly >30)
A1 <3 · A2 3–30 · A3 >30 — cross-ref mg/g only when a KDIGO threshold is quoted (ACR 20 mg/mmol ≈ 200 mg/g).

## CKD definition
eGFR <60 and/or ACR ≥3 mg/mmol, sustained >3 months. A single result is not a diagnosis.
